# Equity Valuation and Strategic Analysis
## Project Overview
This repository contains a full-scale fundamental and relative valuation of [Company Name] ([Ticker]), conducted as of July 1, 2024. The objective of this project is to determine the intrinsic value of the firm's equity to provide a data-backed investment recommendation (Buy/Sell/Hold). 

Beyond pure financial modeling, this project integrates broader strategic, macroeconomic, and industry-level analyses to contextualize the numbers, bridging the gap between raw data and actionable business intelligence.

## Repository Architecture
* ` excel_model/`: Contains the interconnected Pro-forma and DCF valuation model.
* ` report/`: Contains the comprehensive Top-Down Analysis and Valuation Report.


## Methodology & Approach

### 1. Strategic & Macroeconomic Analysis (Top-Down)
* **Macro Environment:** Evaluation of global and Australian economic indicators (GDP, inflation, RBA monetary policy, demographics) and their direct impact on product supply/demand elasticity.
* **Industry Dynamics:** Assessment of market structure, competitive moats, sector profitability, and key stakeholder relationships (suppliers/customers).
* **Firm-Specific Strategy:** Analysis of business lines, corporate governance, ESG integration, and risk factors driving performance.

### 2. Financial Modeling & DCF Valuation
* **Data Structuring:** Cleaned and adjusted historical financial data (2020-2024) from LSEG Workspace.
* **Pro-forma Forecasting:** Five-year projections (2025-2029) utilizing regression methodology for revenue forecasting.
* **Intrinsic Valuation:** Executed both Free Cash Flow to Equity (FCFE) and Free Cash Flow to the Firm (FCFF) models. Discount rates derived via CAPM, with beta calculated by regressing historical share returns against market indices.

### 3. Relative Valuation (Multiples)
* **Peer Analysis:** Justified selection of peer firms for comparative multiple analysis (Trailing/Leading P/E, PEG, P/B).
* **Sector Benchmarking:** Evaluated firm multiples against broader industry indices to isolate firm-specific premium/discount factors.
* **Reconciliation:** Synthesized conflicting signals between fundamental and relative metrics to form a cohesive final thesis.

## Core Competencies Demonstrated
* **Financial Modeling:** DCF, FCFE, FCFF, WACC calculation.
* **Data Analysis & Statistics:** Regression analysis, Beta estimation, data structuring.
* **Strategic Consulting:** Market structure analysis, competitive positioning, ESG evaluation.
* **Commercial Awareness:** Synthesizing macroeconomic trends into tangible business impacts.
